# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders of the WyvenCraft community pledge to make participation in our server and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior ✅

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and kind to other players
- Showing empathy and understanding
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Helping new players learn and grow
- Celebrating others' achievements
- Reporting issues and bugs responsibly

### Unacceptable Behavior ❌

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Griefing, stealing, or destroying others' work without permission
- Using exploits, hacks, or cheats
- Sharing personal or private information without consent
- Spamming chat or voice channels
- Inappropriate or offensive usernames, skins, or builds
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Server administrators and moderators are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Moderators have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject messages, builds, and other content that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All areas of the WyvenCraft Minecraft server
- Official WyvenCraft Discord server
- GitHub repository (issues, discussions, PRs)
- Any other official WyvenCraft community spaces
- When representing WyvenCraft in public spaces

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the moderation team:

### In-Game

Use the `/report` command or contact an online staff member

### Discord

Contact a moderator or administrator directly, or use the designated reporting channel

### GitHub

For violations on GitHub, please email the repository maintainers or use GitHub's reporting features

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All moderators are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Moderators will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for violations:

### 1. Warning

**Community Impact:** Minor violation or first-time offense

**Consequence:** A private written warning, providing clarity around the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** Repeated violations or a single serious violation

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from the server or community spaces for a specified period. No interaction with the community during this time.

### 3. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Severe or repeated violations, including sustained inappropriate behavior

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from all WyvenCraft community spaces and the server.

## Appeals

If you believe you have been unfairly banned or punished:

1. Wait at least 7 days after the punishment
2. Submit an appeal through the designated appeals process
3. Be honest and respectful in your appeal
4. Understand that appeals are not guaranteed to be accepted
